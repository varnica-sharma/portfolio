IEEE CIEES 2025

Benchmarking Machine Learning Techniques in Under-Resourced Contexts

Analysis of Public Perceptions of Government Policies from South Karnataka Reddit Discourse

6th International Conference on Communications, Information, Electronic and Energy Systems November 26-28, 2025 • Ruse, Bulgaria

Abstract: This study employs established Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to gauge public sentiment on government policies in South Karnataka, India. We benchmarked traditional ML models (SVM, Decision Tree, Naïve Bayes, KNN, Random Forest) against deep learning (DistilBERT) under low-data conditions. SVM achieved 41.5% accuracy, slightly outperforming DistilBERT (40.4%). For thematic clustering, K-Means demonstrated 94.6% accuracy with highly coherent topics, establishing the first performance baseline for sentiment analysis of regional Indian public policy discourse.
